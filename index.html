What is Model United Nations?

Model United Nations (MUN) is an educational simulation that mirrors the workings of the United Nations. Students take on the roles of delegates representing different countries, engaging in debates, negotiations, and drafting resolutions to address pressing global issues. It's an opportunity to step into the shoes of world leaders and diplomats, experiencing international relations firsthand.

Why Participate?

Research & Analysis

Build strong analytical and research abilities through in-depth policy exploration.

Public Speaking

Gain confidence in public speaking and debating on the global stage.

Leadership

Develop leadership and teamwork qualities through collaborative problem-solving.

Global Awareness

Gain a deep awareness of global events and international relations.

Our Club Objectives

The MVGRCE MUN Club is dedicated to fostering well-rounded individuals prepared to engage with the complexities of our global community.

Enhance Communication

Boost confidence and clarity through debates and speeches, developing articulate expression of complex ideas.

Promote Diplomacy

Practice persuasion, negotiation, and compromise to build consensus and find common ground.

Encourage Research

Foster analytical thinking through policy drafting and comprehensive research on global issues.

Build Leadership

Provide opportunities to lead as Secretariat or Executive Board members, guiding fellow delegates.

Develop Global Awareness

Understand world politics and pressing geopolitical challenges shaping our interconnected world.
